## Pricing Psychology

### Don't Charge Hourly (At First)

**Hourly:** $50/hour × 10 hours = $500
**Project:** $2000 for "automated content system"

Same work, 4x revenue. Price on VALUE, not time.

### The Ladder

1. **Free:** First 1-2 clients for testimonials
2. **Low:** $100-300 (get confidence)
3. **Medium:** $500-1000 (portfolio building)
4. **High:** $2000+ (established expert)
